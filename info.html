About The LEGO Database

Your guide to exploring LEGO sets!

Purpose

The LEGO Database was created to help LEGO enthusiasts search, browse, and explore sets from all popular themes like Marvel, Star Wars, and Ninjago in one place.

How to Use

  1. Use the search bar on the homepage to find a LEGO set.
  2. Navigate through the catalogue to explore sets by theme.
  3. Visit the contact page for any inquiries or feedback.

Features

  • Quick and easy search.
  • Browse sets by theme or category.
  • Responsive design for any device.
